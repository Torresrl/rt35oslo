Traveling to Oslo

Oslo is in the Southern part of Norway, well connected by all means of transportation.

International flights are to Oslo Airport Gardermoen which is only 20min away from the city center via airport express train. All major airlines, as well as several lowcost, fly to Oslo.

Transportation in Oslo

Nice apps to have:

  • Flytoget (Airport Express train)
  • Ruter (All public transport in Oslo, busses, subway and tram).
  • VY (Train)
  • Bolt (taxi)
  • Uber (Taxi)

From Oslo Airport it’s suggested to take the train. You can either take the AirPort Express (20€) or the regional train (10€). AirPort Express is slightly faster and a bit more comfortable. Tickets can be bought on sight on Ticket Terminals, or through the apps.

Take the train to Oslo Central Station (Oslo S).

It’s highly recommended to only use taxi through the apps mentioned, as the price and route is set and reasonable. It can get quite expensive if you just get one from the street.
