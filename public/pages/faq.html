FAQ

What is the legal drinking age in Norway?

18 for beer, wine and cider. 20 for spirits such as vodka, gin and rum.

Is it allowed to consume alcohol outside?

In general – no.

How much alcohol is included in the event?

Alcohol is crazy expensive in Norway, but we will make sure that you are all well treated with lots of quality beer and spirits. However, you should expect to pay for cocktails and some of the alcohol.

What should I bring to the event?

June in Oslo is usually warm and delightful, but there could be the occasional shower of rain. Check the weather before travelling. We recommend a light jacket, swim pants and all the Round Table gear you have.

What is the “homeparty” planned for Friday?

At the homeparty you will be mixed together and spend parts of the night at the home of one of our members here in Oslo. You will be treated with local food, drinks and lots of fun. After dinner we will all meet up in the center of Oslo.

What is the “City walk” planned for Saturday?

The City Walk is a guided tour around some of the most important sights Oslo can offer. The royal castle, the parliament and such. We will ofcourse also enjoy a cold beer.
